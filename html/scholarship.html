Astrono[ME]
2/ 14 / 2023

We provide yearly scholarships to undergraduate and graduate students as we continuously strive to improve our research and technology. Five individuals are annually offered a $10,000 grant to work with our teams in areas of their choosing as well as a ride to the ISS.

Past fields of interest have included cryogenic fuel technology, optimal seat construction, and water landing gear. All scholarship winners will automatically gain a seat on a flight for no extra charge for the advancement of their chosen field.
