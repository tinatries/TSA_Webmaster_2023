Our Pledge to be Carbon Neutral, Fuel Efficient, and Environmentally Conscious

John Smith
1/ 06 / 2023

At AstronoME, we care deeply about the environment and our impact on it. Thatâ€™s why we remain transparent about our impact and do all we can to reduce it while keeping the safety and comfort of our customers a top priority.

Our aeroponic food systems allow us to use 98% less water than traditional farming, helping the planet by reducing the weight of our ship and therefore requiring the use of less fuel. We never use freeze-dried food, in part because of the amount of plastic waste it requires.

Our cryogenic fuel is hydrogen-based and emits only warm water and air as byproducts, as compared to other fuels like gasoline that emit harmful chemicals like nitrogen oxide. We also use reinforced fuel tanks to ensure against leaks.
